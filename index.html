Elon Musk

Biography

Elon Musk was born in South Africa in 1971. Over the years, he has become known for his ambitious projects and willingness to take risks. From software startups to electric cars and rockets, he has always pushed boundaries.

Companies

SpaceX

SpaceX designs, develops, and launches advanced rockets and spacecraft. Musk founded it with the goal of reducing space transportation costs and enabling the colonization of Mars.

Tesla

Tesla has transformed the auto industry with electric vehicles and sustainable energy products. Musk's leadership has made Tesla a symbol of innovation and clean energy.
